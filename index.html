How every PGI score is built

The same three steps drive both the State index (PGI 2.0) and the District index (PGI-D) — only the totals, domains and band-setting differ.

1

Score each indicator

Each indicator earns its achieved value × its weight. The benchmark for every indicator is fixed in advance.

e.g. Grade 3 Maths (weight 30): if 62.4% are proficient → 30 × 0.624 = 18.7 points.
2

Sum to domain & total

Indicator points add up to domain/category scores; those add up to the overall total — 1000 for States, 600 for Districts.

UP State: 81.4 + 52.7 + 73.4 + 231.2 + 68.6 + 61.8 = 569.14 / 1000.
3

Place into a grade band

The total maps to one of 10 grade bands, from Utkarsh (top) to Akanshi-3. States and districts are graded, not ranked.

569.14 → State band Akanshi-1.
